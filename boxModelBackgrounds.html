The styles have changed to make each box look better.

Pink - This has a margin of 20px on each side. This means it is 20 pixels away from any other divs within the body. I changed the height of the div to leave more margin space.

Turquoise - This has a margin of 50 px on all sides. Note that the top will be 50px plus the 20px from the bottom of the Pink div above it.

Yellow - This has margins of different sizes using the syntax - margin: 0px 10px 80px 100px; note that since the top margin is 0px, it will only take on the 50px from the bottom of the Turquoise div above it.

Red - The margins here have the same at the top and bottom and the same on the left and right, using the syntax of margin: 10px 500px. Again, note that the top margin is only 10px, but it takes on the bottom margin of 100px from the yellow div above it.
